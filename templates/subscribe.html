Frequently asked

Can I cancel any time?

Yes. There are no contracts or lock-in periods. Cancel from your account settings and you won't be charged again.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit and debit cards via Stripe. PayPal support is coming soon.

Can I switch tiers later?

Absolutely. Upgrade or downgrade any time from your account page. Prorated billing applies for mid-cycle changes.

Is there a free trial?

The Frost tier is permanently free — no trial period, no expiry. Paid tiers do not currently include a trial, but that may change.
